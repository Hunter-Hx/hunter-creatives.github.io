Mum, Don't Cry

From the collection: Silent Cries And Other Poems

Mum, don't cry
For the pain that we are going through
We'll soon overcome this trial
And lay these burdens to rest

It hurts me to see you cry
And pains me to see your tears overflow
But please mum, don't cry
For the agony will soon wane.

They labeled you a beggar
Just because you wanted us to feel better
But mum, don't cry
My love for you remains true
And my plans for you are nothing but good.

I hate to see your tears
For it clouds my happiness and dims my hope
So please mum, don't cry.

I feel your burden, mum
And it hurts to see you alone
But please mum, don't cry,
For we hope for a brighter tomorrow
Where the stars light our path
And success is ours forevermore
So mum, don't cry.

Liters of tears have overflowed
And I will never tire of wiping them away
Because you are my lovely mum
Even in tears, I still love you
But please mum, assure me you won't cry

Mum, don't cry
For tomorrow will be better
And we'll share bread and butter.
