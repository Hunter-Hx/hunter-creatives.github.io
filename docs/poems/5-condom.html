Codom

From the collection: Silent Cries And Other Poems

The walls speak louder
When the heart is quiet,
And silence stretches longer
Than memory allows.

Footsteps fade into distance,
Names dissolve into air,
But echoes remain,
Whispering what we dare not say.

For in stillness,
Truth breathes.
And in silence,
The soul remembers.
